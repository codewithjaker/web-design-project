Pest Control Strategies

Integrated Pest Management (IPM) with biological, mechanical, and organic controls.

Biological Control

Using natural predators like ladybugs, lacewings, and parasitic wasps to control aphids, mites, and caterpillars.

  • Release Trichogramma for stem borer
  • Use neem oil as repellent
  • Encourage beneficial insects

Organic Remedies

Homemade sprays and traps: garlic-chili extract, soap water, pheromone traps for fruit flies.

  • Neem kernel extract
  • Garlic-pepper spray
  • Sticky traps

Chemical Control

Safe and targeted pesticides as last resort, following recommended dosage and safety periods.

  • Carbofuran for soil pests
  • Cypermethrin for bollworm
  • Always use PPE

Monitoring & Detection

Regular field scouting, yellow pan traps, and light traps to identify pest population thresholds.

  • Check leaf undersides
  • Count insects per plant
  • Economic threshold level

Cultural Practices

Crop rotation, intercropping, resistant varieties, and proper spacing to reduce pest outbreaks.

  • Rotate rice with legumes
  • Plant marigold as repellent
  • Use certified seeds
